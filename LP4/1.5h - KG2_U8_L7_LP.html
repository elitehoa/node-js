Student Book p32 - 1. Listen to the story.
0
Stage aim
  • Listen gives students practise of comprehension of the story.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for careful listening and following of the story.
Context
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books.
