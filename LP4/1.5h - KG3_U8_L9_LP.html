Student Book p34 - Talk about the pictures.
0
Stage aim
  • Talk about the pictures activates vocabulary recall. Students identify the transport modes and numbers in the review section to ensure they can name them before the listening task.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for accurate naming of the vehicles (Taxi, Train, Boat, Bus, Walking, Bike) and correct reading of the numbers (21, 12, 18) when prompted.
Context
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs

0
Stage aim
  • Ask and answer assesses speaking fluency and grammar application. Students interview a partner using the target question and reply with their personal mode of transport.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for clear pronunciation of the question and the response "I go by [bus/car]" or "I walk."
