Student Book p25 - Listen and count.
0
Stage aim
  • Listen and count introduces the auditory sequence of numbers 30–40. Students listen to the track and track the numbers to learn the correct pronunciation and order.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for accurate tracking of the numbers with a finger and clear choral repetition of "thirty, thirty-one..." etc.
Context
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs

Numbers Book p91 - Add or take away.
0
Stage aim
  • Add or take away introduces calculation using visual support. Students use the number line to 'jump' forward (add) or backward (take away) to solve the sums.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct use of the number line (using a finger to hop) and writing the correct answers (32, 36, 36, 38).
