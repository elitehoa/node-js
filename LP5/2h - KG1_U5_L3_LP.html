Student Book p13 - Listen and point.
0
Stage aim
  • Listen and point checks understanding of word meaning and sound as students match what they hear to the right image. It also strengthens first-sound awareness by isolating the target sounds before giving examples of words beginning with those sounds.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for careful listening, correct pointing to the target words as they are heard.
Context
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs

Student Book p13 - Draw the lines to match.
0
Stage aim
  • Draw the lines to match further strengthens first-sound awareness. It demonstrates early phonics control by linking each sound to the picture that begins with it. It also helps students improve their fine motor skills by drawing the lines to connect the letters and pictures.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for accurate identification of the letter and sound, correct identification of the pictures and correct isolation of their first sound, and neat tracing of the lines.
Context
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books.
