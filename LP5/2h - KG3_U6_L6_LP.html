Student Book p15 - 1. Listen and read. Count and write.
0
Stage aim
  • Listen and read exposes students to the usage of How many…?, models counting by 5s and allows them to practise their literacy skills. Count and write further reviews the numbers 1-50 and counting in tens, and gives them a further opportunity to practise number formation.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for students following the text (with their eyes or finger) while they listen, clear pronunciation, and correct identification of each word while they read. Look for accurate counting by five, correct identification of the numbers 1-50, and correct numbers written neatly on the lines.
Context
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs
