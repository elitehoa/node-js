Student Book p32 - Listen & point
0
Stage aim
  • Listen and point shows they match what they hear to the right word.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for careful listening and correct pointing.
Context
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs
