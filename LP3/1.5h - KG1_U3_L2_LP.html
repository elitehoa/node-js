Student Book p20 - 1. Listen and point.
0
Stage aim
  • Listen and point checks understanding of word meaning and sound as students match what they hear to the right image.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for careful listening and correct pointing to the appropriate image.
Context
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs

Activity Book p46 - Ask and answer ‘what’s this?’
0
Stage aim
  • Ask and answer ‘what’s this?’ provides freer practice of the target language, improves fluency and strengthens students’ awareness of turn taking during pairwork.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for accurate pronunciation, stress and intonation, careful listening, accurate use of the target language and key structure, and natural turn-taking with classmates.
Context
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs
