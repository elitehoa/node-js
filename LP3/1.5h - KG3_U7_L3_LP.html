Numbers Book p99 - Trace.
0
Stage aim
  • Trace develops fine motor control and numeracy. Students trace the large digits (10, 20, 30, 40) under the job hats to reinforce the formation of multiples of ten.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for neat tracing of the numbers 10, 20, 30, and 40 within the grey lines.
Numbers Book p99 - 2. Write and trace.
0
Stage aim
  • Write and trace extends the number sequence. Students trace the numbers 50, 60, 70 and then write the full sequence (10-40) independently on the lines provided.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct formation of the digits 10, 20, 30, 40 on the empty lines and accurate tracing of the higher numbers (50, 60, 70).
Numbers Book p100 - 1. Look and write.
0
Stage aim
  • Look and write connects digits to written words in a vocational context. Students identify the job character and write the corresponding number word (twenty, thirty, forty, fifty, sixty) next to them.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct spelling and legible handwriting of the number words matching the digits on the characters' shirts (e.g., writing "forty" next to the footballer).
