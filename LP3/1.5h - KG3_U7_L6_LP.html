Student Book p23 - Talk about the pictures. Make predictions.
0
Stage aim
  • Talk about the pictures activates prior knowledge and visual literacy. Students describe the scene (cat in tree, dog barking) and identify the job characters to predict the plot.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for recognition of the "police officer" and "vet" uniforms and simple predictions about how they will help the cat.
Context
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs

0

Numbers Book p102 - Add and write.
0
Stage aim
  • Add and write develops calculation skills using visual support. Students count the base sets (e.g., 30 apples) and add the group of 10 to find the total (40).
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct calculation of the sums (30+10=40, 40+10=50, 50+10=60, 60+10=70) and writing the answers on the lines.
