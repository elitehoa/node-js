Numbers Book p105 - Complete the picture.
0
Stage aim
  • Complete the picture reinforces shape recognition and fine motor skills. Students trace the dotted lines to complete the triangle tree, the square boxes on the truck, and the circular tree.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for neat tracing of the specific geometric forms (triangle, square, circle) within the scene.
Numbers Book p105 - 2. Count and write.
0
Stage aim
  • Count and write develops visual classification and sorting. Students find and count all the examples of each shape (circle, square, triangle, rectangle) in the truck scene and write the totals.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for accurate counting (e.g., counting the sun, wheels, and tree top as circles) and writing the correct number in the corresponding box.
Numbers Book p106 - 1. Trace and say the shape.
0
Stage aim
  • Trace and say reviews the names of 2D shapes. Students trace the outline of the square, triangle, rectangle, and circle while naming them aloud.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct formation of the shapes and clear pronunciation of "square," "triangle," "rectangle," and "circle."
Numbers Book p106 - Draw.
0
Stage aim
  • Draw assesses the ability to produce shapes independently. Students draw a solid version of the shape in the coloured box next to the tracing.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for recognizable drawings of the target shapes showing accurate proportions.
Numbers Book p106 - 3. Count the sides and corners. Write.
0
Stage aim
  • Count sides and corners introduces geometric properties. Students count the straight lines (sides) and points (corners) of each shape and write the number in the grid.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct analysis: Square (4 sides, 4 corners), Triangle (3, 3), Rectangle (4, 4), Circle (0/1 curved line, 0 corners).
