Numbers Book p87 - Colour the books on the top shelf
0
Stage aim
  • Colour reinforces number recognition and listening skills. Students identify specific numbers on the books and apply the correct colour according to the instructions.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct identification of the numbers 30–40 and accurate colouring of the books (e.g., book 33 is red).
Numbers Book p87 - 2. Ask and answer
0
Stage aim
  • Ask and answer provides controlled speaking practice. Students work in pairs to identify the numbers and say the corresponding colour, checking their understanding.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for clear pronunciation of the numbers and colours, and correct matching of the two concepts.
Numbers Book p87 - 3. Write the numbers in order
0
Stage aim
  • Write the numbers develops numeracy and sequencing skills. Students write the missing numbers on the bottom shelf to complete the sequence from 30 to 40.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct numerical order and legible formation of the numbers on the book spines.
Numbers Book p88 - 1. Choose a colour
0
Stage aim
  • Choose a colour allows for personalization and reviews colour vocabulary. Students select a specific colour to focus on for the counting task.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for clear pronunciation of the chosen colour name and focused attention on that specific colour of note.
Numbers Book p88 - Count
0
Stage aim
  • Count develops numeracy skills within the context of the lesson theme. Students count the number of musical notes of their chosen colour to find the total.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for accurate counting (one-to-one correspondence) of the musical notes in the picture.
Numbers Book p88 - 3. Write
0
Stage aim
  • Write provides controlled practice in number formation. Students write the total number of notes they counted to complete the activity.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct formation of the number and accuracy in the count (matching the visual evidence).
