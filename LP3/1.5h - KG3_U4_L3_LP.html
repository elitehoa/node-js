Numbers Book p93 - Match.
0
Stage aim
  • Match develops geometric recognition in real-world objects. Students look at the food items and draw lines connecting them to the correct 3D shape category (cylinder, sphere, or cone).
Evidence of learning
  • Look for logical connections: carrots and ice cream to 'cone'; peas and cherries to 'sphere'; banana and ham to 'cylinder'.
Numbers Book p94 - 1. Find the 3D shapes.
0
Stage aim
  • Find the 3D shapes develops visual grouping and observation skills. Students scan the page to identify different food items that share the same geometric properties.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for students pointing to or circling the items that match the teacher's prompt (e.g., "Find all the cylinders/cans").
Numbers Book p94 - Count and write.
0
Stage aim
  • Count and write combines geometry with numeracy. Students count the total number of items for each shape category and write the correct digit next to the shape icon.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for accurate counting of the sets (e.g., counting all the berries, melons and oranges together as 'spheres') and writing the correct totals.
