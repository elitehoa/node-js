Numbers Book p93 - 1. Count the windows.
0
Stage aim
  • Count the windows allows students to practise some of the target language while strengthening their counting skills.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for accurate counting of the windows, and correct pronunciation of the numbers.
Numbers Book p93 - 2. Look and draw windows.
0
Stage aim
  • Look and draw windows strengthens recognition of the target language with number-picture matching.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct pencil grip when drawing the windows and the correct number of windows drawn.
Numbers Book p94 - 1. Make 10. Look and match.
0
Stage aim
  • Make 10. Look and match reinforces the numbers 1-10 and provides controlled practice of number bonds to ten.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for clear lines between the dominoes and correct matching of the dominoes that together make a total of ten.
