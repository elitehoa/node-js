Student Book p14 - 3 & 4. When do you brush your teeth? Tick.
0
Stage aim
  • When do you…? Tick reinforces the key language from the lesson and is the starting point for a discussion about when students do the actions, adding an element of personalisation to the lesson.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct identification of the target actions and the parts of the day represented in the pictures at the bottom, neat ticks within the boxes, and students volunteering information about when they wash their hands and brush their teeth.

Activity Book p62 - 2. Match.
0
Stage aim
  • Match helps build connections between the target actions and when they should be carried out, and strengthens fine motor skills.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct identification of pictures, correct identification of when the actions should be carried out (e.g. “brush teeth” matches the picture eating sweets, and “wash hands” matches the picture of the child painting etc), and neat lines to connect the pictures.
