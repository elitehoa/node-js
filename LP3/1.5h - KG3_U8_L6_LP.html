Student Book p31 - Talk about the pictures. Make predictions.
0
Stage aim
  • Talk about the pictures activates prior knowledge and sets the scene. Students describe the different environments (city vs. countryside) and the transport they see to predict what the children will encounter.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for simple descriptions like "city," "bus," "trees," or "bike" and pointing to the characters walking.
Context
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs

Numbers Book p108 - Match.
0
Stage aim
  • Match links large numbers to their written form. Students draw lines connecting the vehicle with the digit (e.g., Bus 19) to the corresponding number word (nineteen).
Evidence of learning
  • Look for accurate lines connecting: 19-nineteen, 64-sixty-four, 55-fifty-five, 41-forty-one, 37-thirty-seven.
Context
