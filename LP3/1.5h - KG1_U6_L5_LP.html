Student Book p22 - Listen, point & say
0
Stage aim
  • Listen, Point and Say checks understanding of word meaning and sound. Listen and point shows they match what they hear to the right image. Say practises first-step pronunciation.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for careful listening, correct pointing, clear sounds, and confident repetition.
Context
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs

Activity Book p68 - Cut, sort and stick
0
Stage aim
  • Cut, sort and stick prepares students for freer practice of the target language by placing the cut out animals in the home or the barn. Sorting reinforces comprehension of where animals naturally belong. Stick aids fine motor skills, improving hand-eye co-ordination and developing precision.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct handling of scissors and careful cutting out, sorting into correct domestic and non-domestic categories pictorially and the ability to direct the glue where intended.
Context
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction.
