Warm up/Review Game⚡
10
Student Book p.19 - A Find the eight hidden pictures
15
Stage aim
  • Find the eight hidden pictures reviews how to identify and spell the unit’s key words.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct identification of the objects in the picture.
Context
  • “Class, we have a problem. Some robbers came in the night and they’ve taken some of our school things. I need you to help me find them.”
Procedure
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs

Student Book p.20 - Speaking Toss a coin. Then ask and answer.
30
Stage aim
  • This Speaking activity helps students practise their speaking skills in relation to the target language. This strengthens fluency, demonstrates their ability to follow instructions and gives them an opportunity to demonstrate turn taking skills.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for students moving one space if the coin lands on heads and two spaces for tails, the correct identification of the objects in the pictures, and the correct use of ‘this’ and ‘that’.
Context
  • “I’m so relieved our items have been returned,. Can you work together to help me check we’ve got everything back?”
Procedure
