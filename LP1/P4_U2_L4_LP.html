Student Book p.19 - A Fill in the blanks
10
Stage aim
  • Fill in the blanks reviews prior learning and checks comprehension as students recall the jobs and job action keywords they have learnt.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for confident recall, correct sentence structures and spelling.
Context
  • “Do you want to go to the future? I do! Look at the pictures. Can you tell me the jobs? Let's listen carefully to the children... what do they want to be?”
Procedure
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs

Student Book p.20 - Presentation Think about your dream job. Then draw and fill in the chart. Present your dream job to the class.
40
Stage aim
  • The presentation task gives students a chance to apply their learning to themselves, choosing their own dream job, what they will do and who they want to work for.
