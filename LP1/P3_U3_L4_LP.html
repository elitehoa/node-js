Student Book p.31 - A Look and write
10
Stage aim
  • Look and write serves as a review of the unit’s vocabulary. Students identify the numbered food items in the picture and write them on the list to consolidate spelling.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct identification of the food items from the scene and accurate spelling of the six target vocabulary words.
Context
  • “Our fridge is empty. It’s time for a trip to the big supermarket! Can you help me write a shopping list?”
Procedure
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs

Student Book p.32 - Speaking Presentation Our Fridges
30
Stage aim
  • Our Fridges is a speaking task that synthesizes the unit's vocabulary and grammar. Students use the language in a personalized, communicative context through pair work and a final presentation.
