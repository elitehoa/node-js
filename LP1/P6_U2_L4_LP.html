Student Book p.19 - A Look & write
10
Stage aim
  • Look and write gives controlled writing practice with past-tense verbs and feeling adjectives, helping students describe amusement-park activities with accurate word choice and spelling.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for correct word choices, complete sentences that match the pictures, past-tense verbs and adjectives used accurately, and neat, legible handwriting.
Context
  • “I liked the amusement park so much… I went to another one! What animals can you see at the petting zoo? Look at the people here… how do they feel? I want to know all about their day. Let’s help them finish the sentences about what they did and how they felt.”
Procedure
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs

Student Book p.20 - Presentation My Amusement Park Minibook
