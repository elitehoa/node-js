Student Book p.13 - A Listen, point, and say
10
Stage aim
  • Listen, point, and say checks understanding of new vocabulary.. Listen and point shows students can match what they hear to the right image. Say practises first-step pronunciation.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for careful listening, correct pointing, confident repetition and clear pronunciation.
Context
  • “Class, I’m not sure I want to be a teacher anymore. I want to do something different. I love drawing (mime) and acting (mime) and sports too! What jobs do you like? Let's take a look!'”
Procedure
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs

Student Book p.14 - Chant
10
Stage aim
  • The chant allows students to practise the sentence structures and new vocabulary they have learnt.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for confident and correct pronunciation and expression.
Context
  • “Amazing work, class! You have given me so many ideas. There are so many jobs! I want to do them all! But... I think... teaching you is my favourite thing. You are an amazing class! What do you think? Are you the best class in the school?!”
Procedure
  • Heads Up (Reading): Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and help Ss to understand the text by asking questions, practicing skimming (reading for gist), scanning (reading for specific information) and detail (reading for comprehension). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down (Listen and Chant): Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together (Video Chant): Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs
