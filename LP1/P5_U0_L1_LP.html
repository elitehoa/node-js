Student Book p1-2 - A Listen and say
0
Stage aim
  • Listen and say checks understanding of continent names and their sounds. Say practises first-step pronunciation.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for careful listening, clear sounds on longer names, and confident repetition.
Context
  • “I met some new friends but I forgot where they live! Can you help me remember? Let’s listen and look at the map together”
Procedure
  • Heads Up: Focus the students’ attention on the CPT and present relevant features of the language (MFP). Then demonstrate the activity with TA/students. Heads Down: Ss open their books. Instruct and ask ICQs. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Heads Together: Demo/Instruct and ask ICQs, including setting behaviour expectations. Then monitor, providing support and stretch where needed. Conduct activity feedback and error correction. Reward moment Check behaviour expectations and reward with APOS or remind for 4Rs

Break [10m] - C. Listen. Then ask and Answer
0
Stage aim
  • Listen checks understanding of descriptive vocabulary related to the five senses. Ask and answer gives students controlled practice using the target question form to ask and respond to simple questions.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for accurate use of question forms, clear descriptive answers with correct sense verbs (sounds/looks/feels/smells/tastes), and consistent reference to details in the picture.
Context

Break [10m] - D. Look and learn
0
Stage aim
  • Look and learn introduces units of measurement through simple visuals and supports recognition of when weight, distance or length is being shown.
Evidence of learning
