Apollo Lesson Plan

Warm up/Review Game
10
Reader Page 1 - A Prepare 1. Choose 1-2 key scenes as a class.
0
Stage aim
  • Prepare allows students to build confidence by working in groups. Choose 1- 2 key scenes as a class gives students ownership of the task by involving them in the decision making.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for active participation in the decision making, following the task procedure.
Reader Page 1 - 2. Assign roles in groups.
0
Stage aim
  • Assign roles in groups ensures all students are aware of their own role.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for decision making and fairness and clarity of roles.
Reader Page 1 - 3. Prepare your masks.
0
Stage aim
  • Prepare your masks develops creative skills, allowing students to create something based on their understanding of the characters.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for students staying on task, creating masks which are relevant to the story, careful use of scissors and materials used to make the mask, and active participation.
Reader Page 1 - B Practise 1. Practise performing lines in groups following A.
0
Stage aim
  • Practise allows students to prepare and practise for their performance. Practise performing lines in groups following A builds confidence, allowing students to produce their lines in a supportive environment.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for teamwork, turn taking, authentic pronunciation and active participation from all students.
Reader Page 1 - 2. Focus on group work rather than individual performances.
0
Stage aim
  • Focus on group work encourages students to work together and foster collaboration.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for teamwork, turn taking and group members respecting and supporting each other.
