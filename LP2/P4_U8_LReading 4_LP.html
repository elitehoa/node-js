Apollo Lesson Plan

Warm up/Review Game
10
Reader Page 1 - A Prepare 1, Choose 1-2 scenes as a class. 2. Assign roles in groups. 3. Prepare your masks.
0
Stage aim
  • Prepare engages students in collaborative planning by selecting scenes, taking on roles, and creating simple masks, helping them understand characters and key events in the story.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for cooperative group work, appropriate role assignment, engagement in mask preparation, and students contributing ideas.
Reader Page 1 - B. Practice 1. Practice performing lines in groups following A. 2. Focus on group work rather than individual performances.
0
Stage aim
  • Practice develops speaking confidence and fluency by rehearsing lines together, focusing on teamwork, expression, and shared performance.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for clear speaking, group coordination, supportive teamwork, and active participation from all students.
Reader Page 1 - C. Relaxed performance
0
Stage aim
  • Relaxed Performance allows students to demonstrate understanding of the story through expressive group performance, building confidence and enjoyment in using spoken language.
Evidence of learning
  • Look for confident group performances, clear use of story language, cooperation, and positive participation rather than perfection.
