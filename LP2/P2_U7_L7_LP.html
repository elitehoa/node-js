Video Lesson - A. Before Watching
10
Purpose
  • • Activate schema and topic knowledge • Discuss the picture to build interest • Support comprehension with key vocab • Set a clear reason for watching
Teacher should
  • • Highlight picture details (Who/Where/What?) • Ask simple prediction questions • Read any intro text and check meaning • Pre-teach only essential words • Ask 1–2 ICQs to confirm the task
Student outcomes
  • • Ss are oriented to the topic • Ss know what to look for during viewing • Ss feel ready for the video
Video Lesson - B. While Watching
20
Purpose
  • • Develop gist and detail listening • Guide Ss through comprehension tasks • Reinforce target structure or vocabulary
Teacher should
  • • Give a clear instruction (“Watch, then answer”) • Use gestures and ICQs (“Do we talk while watching?”) • Play once for gist, again for detail • Pause only when needed • Model the language focus with the TA
Student outcomes
  • • Ss complete T/F, matching, or Q&A tasks • Ss notice the target language in context • Ss stay focused with clear viewing routines
Video Lesson - C. After Watching
20
Purpose
  • • Check final understanding • Move from comprehension to controlled output • Connect theme to Ss’ own experiences
Teacher should
  • • Guide Ss through SB tasks using prompts • Model with TA and check ICQs • Monitor closely and support spelling/vocab • Review answers as a class • Follow with a short pair task
Student outcomes
  • • Ss complete simple speaking/writing tasks • Ss use new language meaningfully • Ss personalise the video topic
